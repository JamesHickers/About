Saz

Saz is a powerful, high-performance Roblox executor developed by 4zx16 and Blood Gang™️, designed to supercharge your Roblox scripting experience with built-in tools and ease-of-use that stand out in the community.

Core Features

  • Executor: Fast and reliable script execution with advanced injection methods optimized for Roblox.
  • F3X Tools: Integrated auto-updating building and editing tools that support custom modifications and flexibility.
  • NPC Tools: Custom AI and NPC interaction modules to create complex gameplay mechanics easily.
  • User Interface: Clean, modern UI designed with input from christopherdis to keep things sleek and intuitive.
  • Punishment Module: Developed by 7zh14, this feature allows for moderated gameplay management and anti-abuse functionality.
  • Regular Updates: Frequent releases ensure compatibility with Roblox updates and add requested features.
